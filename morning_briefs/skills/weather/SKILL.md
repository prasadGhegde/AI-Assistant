# Weather Skill

Weather is a first-class briefing section, not a side note.

## Inputs

- Configured location name.
- Latitude and longitude.
- Current temperature and apparent temperature.
- General conditions.
- Wind speed and gusts.
- Precipitation probability.
- Cloud cover.

## Output

The spoken weather section should include:

- Current temperature and conditions.
- One short carry or wear tip.
- Rain, wind, sun, snow, or visibility cautions only when useful.
- A graceful transition into the news flow.

## Style

Keep weather practical, quick, cheerful, and slightly playful. It should take 20 to 25 seconds, feel like a capable morning assistant helping someone leave the house prepared, and give a little morning lift without becoming a long forecast.

## Visual Behavior

The dashboard should enter a dedicated weather animation state before news begins. Weather remains synchronized with narration through the presentation timeline.
